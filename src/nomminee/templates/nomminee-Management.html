Frequently Asked Questions

A nominee is a person designated by the account holder to receive the account proceeds in case of the holder's demise. As per the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) guidelines, the nominee acts as a trustee of the legal heirs but does not become the legal owner.

  • One-to-One Relationship: Each account can have only one nominee.
  • Legal Position: A nominee does not inherit ownership but only acts as a trustee.
  • Modification & Cancellation: The account holder can change or remove the nominee anytime.
  • Information Required: Name, relationship, contact details, and address (if required).
